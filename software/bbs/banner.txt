==============================================================
   RESILIENCE HUB — PUBLIC MESSAGE BOARD (TEXT ONLY)
==============================================================
PRIORITIES & QUOTAS (default):
  A — Life Safety .......... Unlimited
  B — Welfare .............. 2 msgs/hour/user
  C — Bulletin ............. 1 msg/2 hours/user
  (Rolling 6-hour window; may tighten during outages)

MESSAGE FORMAT (required headers):
  To: @REGION/CITY/CALLSIGN  (or HUBID, e.g., @WI/HOLMEN/HUB1)
  From: @REGION/CITY/CALLSIGN
  Priority: A|B|C
  Expires: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MMZ
  Via: HF|VHF|POTS|MESH
  Sig: (optional PGP signature block)

TEMPLATES:
  - ICS-213 (condensed): data/templates/ICS-213.txt
  - Welfare check:       data/templates/welfare.txt

POLICY:
  - Legal, noncommercial, emergency-appropriate content only.
  - Amateur RF paths: NO encryption. Signing for authenticity is OK.
  - Be concise. Respect quotas. Operators may defer low-priority traffic.
